80s and 90s

The 80s

The 1980s saw the mainstream introduction of hip-hop, with artists like Run-D.M.C. blending rock and hip-hop, N.W.A. showcasing raw lyrics, and artists like LL Cool J and Public Enemy pushing rap's message. Eric B. & Rakim elevated lyricism with smooth flows and complex rhymes. Hip-hop fashion exploded with Adidas tracksuits, gold chains, and bucket hats, setting trends. DJs scratched vinyl with new techniques, while breakdancers battled with style.
